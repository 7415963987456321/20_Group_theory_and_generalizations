If $G$ is a finite group and $p$ is a \PMlinkname{prime}{Prime} such that $p^k$ divides $|G|$, then there is a \PMlinkname{subgroup}{Subgroup} $H$ of $G$ such that $|H|=p^k$.

This is the first part of several results usually called the Sylow theorems.
