Let $G$ denote the group of rational numbers taking the operation to be addition.  Then for any $\frac{p}{q}\in G$ and $n\in\Z^+$, we have $\frac{p}{nq}\in G$ satisfying $n\frac{p}{nq}=\frac{p}{q}$, so the group is divisible.
